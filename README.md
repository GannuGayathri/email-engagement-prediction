# email-engagement-prediction
In the digital marketing landscape, email remains one of the most effective and affordable communication channels for businesses. However, the success of email campaigns greatly depends on timing. Sending emails without understanding customer behavior often results in low open rates, poor engagement, and reduced marketing impact. This project focuses on analyzing customer data to predict the optimal time to send marketing emails for maximum effectiveness.

The system collects and studies historical email data such as open times, click-through rates, response patterns, user activity periods, and engagement frequency. By applying data analysis techniques and predictive models, meaningful patterns are identified that reveal when users are most likely to interact with emails. Based on these insights, the system recommends personalized or group-based sending times to improve campaign performance.

The main objective of this project is to help businesses make data-driven decisions instead of relying on assumptions or fixed schedules. By optimizing email timing, companies can increase customer engagement, improve conversion rates, and strengthen relationships with their audience. Ultimately, this project demonstrates how intelligent use of data can transform traditional email marketing into a more strategic and customer-focused approach.
